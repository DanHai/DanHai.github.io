Bio

I am Hai Dang, currently a doctoral researcher at the University of Bayreuth in Germany. I'm part of the ZD.B. junior research group: HCIAI where I'm advised by Dr. Daniel Buschek. Before this, I completed my bachelors degree in media informatics and masters degree in computer science at the University Munich in Germany.

My research interests include machine learning, deep learning and human computer interactions. I build interpretable interactive systems to generate creative artifacts from algorithms in ways that are not possible with traditional input methods.

Research Areas

  • Human-Computer Interaction
